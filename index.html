I am an assistant associate professor in Computer Science at the University of Colorado Boulder, with courtesy appointments in Information Science and the ATLAS Institute.

I direct the Superhuman Computing Lab, which conducts research on accessible user interfaces, mobile and wearable interactions, and tangible computing.
